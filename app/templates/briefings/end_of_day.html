How this works

SignalDesk builds a compact evidence pack from today’s extracted files, AI analyses, risks, actions and insights.

The worker sends only that compact evidence pack to OpenAI to produce a polished written narrative.

This is designed as a concise end-of-day executive update, not a raw document dump.
